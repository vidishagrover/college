About

Kiroskar Institute of Advanced Management Studies also known as KIAMS, was established in 1991 in Harihar, Karnataka. It was set up by the Kiroskar Group to put the beliefs of its renowned leader Mr.Shantanurao Laxmanrao Kiroskar to pratice. KIAMS had a second campus in Pune, Maharashtra, in 2010 as an outcome of the enormous accomplishments of its ex-students in the business world.

The 2 years full-time Post-Graduate Program (PGDM) is offered in both of its fully residential campuses and is approved by AICTE. It also offers Management Development Programs (MDP) and Postgraduate Program in Business Analytics and Desicion Science (PGP BA & DS). The admission process in both its campuses is through numerous entrance exams. Both campuses offer topnotch infrastructure, educational superioty, and higher employment ratio compared to other institutes in India.
